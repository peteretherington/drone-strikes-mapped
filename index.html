U.S. Drone Strikes Mapped

A HackerYou bootcamp project combining Josh Begley's Dronestream API with Google Maps

Instructions

  1. Choose a year. NOTE: no strikes were reported in 2003
  2. Choose a country. optional
  3. Click on the map to use it.
  4. Click on a marker to get more info.
  5. Scroll down to see the full results. Search the results by using CMD+F (Mac) or CTRL+F (Windows) and entering the Strike Log #

Useful Info

  • This data base does not contain every drone strike ever launched by the United States, only those that were reported by The Bureau of Investigative Journalism.
  • George Bush was the U.S. President from 2001-2009.
  • Barrack Obama was the U.S. President from 2009-2017.
  • Donald Trump, inaugurated on January 20th of this year, is the current President of the United States. In case you didn't know.
